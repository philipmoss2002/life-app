# Privacy Policy - Short Version

**Household Docs App**

## Your Privacy Matters

This app is designed with privacy as a core principle.

### What We Collect
**Nothing.** We don't collect any personal information.

### Where Your Data Lives
**On your device only.** All documents, files, and information are stored locally on your device using SQLite database.

### What We Share
**Nothing.** Your data never leaves your device. No cloud storage, no servers, no third parties.

### Permissions We Need
- **File Access**: To let you attach files to documents (only files you select)
- **Notifications**: To remind you about upcoming renewal dates (optional)

### Your Control
- View, edit, or delete any document anytime
- Uninstall the app to remove all data
- Your original files are never modified or deleted

### No Tracking
- No analytics
- No advertisements  
- No user accounts
- No internet connection required

---

**Bottom Line:** Your household documents are private. They stay on your device, under your control, always.
